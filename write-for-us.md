## Write for Us – WinkCasa

WinkCasa is a multi-industry news and insights platform covering technology, finance, gaming, and digital entertainment.

We welcome **guest contributors, industry experts, startups, and agencies** to share original insights, announcements, and stories with our audience.

---

## Topics We Accept

You can contribute articles related to:

- Top Companies & Industry Lists  
- Artificial Intelligence & AI Trends  
- Cybersecurity & Digital Safety  
- Cloud Computing & SaaS  
- Payment Solutions (UPI, Fintech, Cross-border)  
- Cryptocurrency & Blockchain  
- Gaming Industry & Esports  
- Micro Drama & Short-Form Content  
- Live Streaming Platforms & Creators  

---

## Contribution Guidelines

- Content must be **original and unpublished**
- Minimum length: **700 words**
- Promotional content must be clearly disclosed
- We may edit content for clarity and formatting

---

## Links & Mentions

- Up to **2 relevant links** allowed
- No spammy or misleading links
- Anchor text must be natural

---

## How to Submit

Please email your article or pitch to:

📧 **contact@winkcasa.com**

Include:
- Article content or topic idea
- Preferred category
- Author name & short bio

---

*WinkCasa reserves the right to accept or reject submissions.*
