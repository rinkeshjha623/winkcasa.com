## Publish With Us – WinkCasa

WinkCasa is a multi-industry news and PR platform covering technology, finance, entertainment, and digital innovation.

We accept **guest posts, sponsored articles, and press releases** from companies, startups, agencies, and individual contributors.

## Content Guidelines

- Articles must be **original and well-written**
- Promotional content must be disclosed as **Sponsored**
- No illegal, adult, or misleading content
- We reserve the right to edit content for clarity

---

## Link Policy

- Up to **2 do-follow links** allowed (contextual)
- Excessive promotional links are not accepted
- Spammy anchors are not allowed
